# Decision Log

Record significant decisions for this AI task. Each entry should be durable enough to survive session changes.

## Format

```markdown
### DEC-YYYYMMDD-NNN: Title
- **Date:**
- **Status:** proposed | accepted | superseded | rejected
- **Context:** What problem or fork triggered this decision?
- **Decision:** What was chosen?
- **Rationale:** Why this option?
- **Alternatives considered:** What else was evaluated?
- **Consequences:** What changes downstream?
- **Links:** Related files, commits, or version hashes
```
